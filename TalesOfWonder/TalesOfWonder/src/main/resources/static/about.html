INTRODUCTION

Tales Of Wonder is a web-based project focused on building a website for people to read online comics featuring multiple categories. The website will be accessible to various users, up to date with stories as well as create a friendly and healthy environment.

Nowadays, there has been a wide range of audience who prefer reading comics online rather than purchasing printed comic books. This mainly due to its user-friendliness and convenience that comics websites have. Although many websites upload comics, it is difficult to keep track of what users read, what chapters remain to be finished or what categories to be divided.

Project: Tales Of Wonder is born to solve those problems.

FEATURES

As a user:

  • 1) Reads new chapters, provides feedback in the comment section as well as rates every story added to the website.
  • 2) Follow favorite comics for new updates as well as keep track of unread chapters (send notifications).
  • 3) Selecting each category to read by choice
  • 4) A glance at popular comics categorized by weeks, months, and days.
  • 5) Create and update their profile.



As a creator:

  • 1) Upload their unique stories to show the world their talents and art style
  • 2) Manage (upload, update, delete) their own comics.
  • 3) Receive generous donations from readers!!!



As an admin:

  • 1) Manage (create, update, delete) profiles of users.
  • 2) Manage (delete) comics (using data collection methods).
  • 3) Manage (insert, update, delete) comments.

MORE INFORMATION

Target User: people over 13 years old.



Environment: Individuals will receive updates via email when a new chapter / comic comes out from their subscriber creators. At their leisure, they will connect to the web site and view their content. Usage patterns are not predictable at this point, though higher volumes are anticipated at evenings but mostly different for each individual. Users will be expected to have an email to receive notification from creators, and are expected to have a browser-enabled device for viewing content. If they have credit cards, they can be used for donation. Admins will require a browser-enabled device for categorizing content and/or viewing system status.



Usability: How easy is it for a customer to use the system?

  • - The system will be accessible to users in a single page visit whereas the website will ensure its user-friendliness by achieving its goal of finding their chosen comics the quickest.
  • - The website is capable of taking intuitive design into account for better user’s experience and memorability.
  • - There will be less error when users utilize the website due to its simplicity and easy-to-use interface..



Performance and scalability: How fast does the system return results?

  • - The website returns the result as responsive as possible (guarantee loading time under 2 minutes).
  • - With a higher number of users and creators accessing the page at the same time (golden time of the day), response time may expect to scale up but no more than 50% average response time.
  • - Transaction time (when donation) is guaranteed under 5 minutes.



Security. How well are the system and its data protected against attacks?

  • - The website will be using an authentication system for better user’s recognition and data protection.
  • - The comics on this website cannot be copied or downloaded to prevent copyright issues.



Reliability (robustness), maintainability, availability

  • - The website has weekly and monthly maintenance to minimize the chance of getting errors.
  • - For small issues, a fixing time of under 30 minutes is expected.
  • - When experiencing critical failure, the system is expected to recover in under 1 hour (during work hours).

Portability and compatibility. Which hardware, operating systems, and browsers, along with their versions does the software run on?

  • - The website is expected to run on any platform that can connect to the internet, and may require an application/browser to display the content and interact.
